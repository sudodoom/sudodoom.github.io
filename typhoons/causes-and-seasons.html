Causes

How do typhoons occur?

Typhoons occur when strong wind pull in moisture from the ocean.
Thunderstorms turn that moisture into heat which causes some air to move to the center
in a circular motion. All the heat from the air moves into the eye of the storm
which makes it swirl and form into a typhoon.

When do typhoons occur?

Typhoons can occur anytime in the year. Most of the time tropical typhoons develop between May and October.
So far in 2019 there has been 7 typhoons and 2 unoffical super typhoons. These typhoons in total have caused
about 9.82 billion dollars USD in damage.
